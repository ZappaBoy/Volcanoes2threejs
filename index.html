Volcanoes 2 threejs

This is a simple website that show volcanoes 3D plot created using Qgis and Qgis2threejs library.
created by MordorTeam. This site was created with the intention of showing the impact
that volcanic eruptions can have on the soil and the environment.
Link to project.

Four volcanoes

We focused about four volcanoes in different countries in the world that we consider suitable
for our studies and that have great importance in the field of volcanology.

Nevado del Ruiz

The Nevado del Ruiz, also known as La Mesa de Herveo, is a volcano located in Colombia, about 129 kilometers west of the capital city Bogotá.

San Cristóbal Volcano

Volcán San Cristóbal is the most active and the highest volcano in Nicaragua at 1,745 m.

Monte Mayon

Mayon Volcano also known as Magayon, is a sacred and active volcano in the province of Albay in the Philippines.

Etna

Mount Etna or Mongibello is an active volcano on the east coast of Sicily, Italy, between the cities of Messina and Catania.
